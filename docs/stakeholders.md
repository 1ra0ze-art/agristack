# Stakeholders — AgriStack

## Primary Stakeholders

### 1. Farmer / Cooperative
- **Who**: Individual potato farmers or registered cooperatives in Musanze District
- **Need**: Post harvest listings, get fair prices, receive confirmed bulk orders
- **Pain**: Price manipulation, no visibility to large buyers, informal agreements
- **Goal**: Increase income by 30–50% through direct sales

### 2. Aggregator / Buyer
- **Who**: Wholesale traders, supermarket procurement officers, export companies, NGOs
- **Need**: Reliable bulk supply of Irish potatoes with quality assurance
- **Pain**: Inconsistent supply, no single platform to discover cooperatives
- **Goal**: Place bulk orders with guaranteed pickup logistics

### 3. Admin (AgriStack Platform Manager)
- **Who**: Platform operations team
- **Need**: Approve listings, monitor order workflow, prevent fraud
- **Pain**: Manual oversight of disputes and fake listings
- **Goal**: Maintain platform integrity and generate audit trails

## Secondary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Interest |
|---|---|
| Musanze District Agriculture Office | Policy compliance, farmer welfare |
| Rwanda Agriculture Board (RAB) | Quality standards for exports |
| Financial institutions (e.g., BPR) | Loan collateral via order confirmations |
| Logistics/transport companies | Pickup coordination at collection sectors |
| Consumers (end market) | Affordable, traceable produce |
